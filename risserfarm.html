You decided you should avoid big crowds of people and found a small farm nearby. Risser Farm. You run out to your car, a black Honda Civic, and get inside. To be extra safe you lock the doors. The streets seem eerily silent, but it was better than the other possibilities.

You hit the gas pedal and make the short drive out of town and to Risser Farm. Risser Farm always had a little market open for fresh produce, but a "CLOSED" sign was taped on the front door. You get out of your car anyway and walk to the front door. You knock a few times, and a little old man hobbles to the door. You explain to him that you are looking for just a little food to take with you on the road. Without so much as a nod, the old man retreats to another room in the market. A few minutes later he's on his way back, unlocks the front door, and hands you a cardboard box full of fresh produce and some jars of jams. You also notice a half of a loaf of white bread nestled in the corner of the box.

"Thank you so, so much sir." you say to the little old man. With a tight smile he bids you good luck and disappears back into his market again. It might not be much but at least it was a start.
